# Image Naming Guide for James

Drop your photos into the folders below with these names. The website will pick them up automatically.

## Gallery Images (assets/images/gallery/)

Name your "after" (finished/clean) shots:
- work-1.jpg — Best overall shot (use for hero too)
- work-2.jpg — Interior dashboard/console cleaned
- work-3.jpg — Back seats cleaned
- work-4.jpg — Trunk/cargo area cleaned
- work-5.jpg — Front seats cleaned
- work-6.jpg — Door panel/detail shot

## Before & After (assets/images/before-after/)

Pair up your before and after shots:
- before-1.jpg — Interior before (dirty floors, debris)
- after-1.jpg — Same angle after cleaning
- before-2.jpg — Another angle before
- after-2.jpg — Same angle after cleaning

## About Photo (assets/images/about/)

- james.jpg — A photo of you (headshot, working on a car, etc.)

## Hero Image (assets/images/hero/)

- hero-bg.jpg — Your best wide shot (a clean car exterior works great)
- og-image.jpg — Same image or similar (used when sharing on social media)

## Tips

- Use your BEST "after" shots for the gallery — that's what sells
- For before/after, try to take photos from the same angle
- Horizontal (landscape) orientation works best for most sections
- Images don't need to be huge — 1200-1600px wide is plenty
