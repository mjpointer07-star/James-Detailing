The person behind the polish

Meet James

I'm James Trinkle, based out of Arvada, Colorado. I started detailing in 2026 after realizing I could turn something I genuinely enjoy into a business. There's something satisfying about taking a car that someone spends hours in every day — without even thinking about how dirty it gets — and making it look brand new again.

I use professional-grade products (the same stuff the pros use, not off-the-shelf sprays) because the results speak for themselves. Whether it's your daily driver or something you baby on weekends, I treat every vehicle like it's my own. Chill, easy to work with, but serious about getting you the results you're after.

When I'm not detailing, you'll probably find me on the soccer field (been playing competitively my whole life), up in the mountains skiing, or at the pool — I've been a lifeguard too. I bring that same energy and discipline to every detail job I take on.
